Policy

Thank you for your interest and visiting our website. We respect and commit to protecting your private information.

The privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and (in certain cases) disclose your personal information.

Protecting personal data and building trust with our customers is very important to us. Therefore, we will use your name and other related information in compliance with the Privacy Policy. We only collect necessary information related to transactions.

We will retain customer information for the period required by law or for specific purposes. You can access and browse the website without providing personal details. At that time, you are anonymous, and we cannot know who you are unless you log into your account.

1. Collection of Personal Information

- We collect, store, and process your information for the purchasing process and for subsequent notifications related to orders, and to provide services, including some personal information: title, name, gender, date of birth, email, address, shipping address, phone number, fax, payment details, credit card details, or bank account details.

- We will use the information you provide to process orders, provide requested services and information through the website, and as per your request.

- Furthermore, we will use this information to manage your account; verify and execute online transactions, recognize website visitors, conduct demographic research, send information including product and service updates. If you do not wish to receive any marketing information from us, you can opt-out at any time.

- We may transfer your name and address to third parties to deliver goods to you (e.g., to courier services or suppliers).

- Your order details are stored by us, but for security reasons, we cannot publicly display them directly. However, you can access this information by logging into your account on the website. Here, you will see your order details, received products, shipped products, and details of emails, bank information, and newsletters you have subscribed to.

- You commit to protecting your personal data and not disclosing it to third parties. We are not responsible for unauthorized use of your password if it is not our fault.

- We may use your personal information for market research. All detailed information will be anonymized and used only for statistical purposes. You can opt-out at any time.

2. Security

- We have appropriate technical and security measures to prevent unauthorized or unlawful access, loss, destruction, or damage to your information.

- We advise you not to share your payment details with anyone via email. We are not responsible for any losses you may incur from sharing your information over the internet or email.

- You must not use any programs, tools, or other means to interfere with or alter the system's data structure. It is strictly prohibited to distribute, propagate, or encourage any activities aimed at interfering with, destroying, or infiltrating the website's data. Any violations will result in the revocation of all rights and may lead to legal prosecution if necessary.

- All transaction information will be kept confidential, but in cases where law enforcement agencies require it, we must provide this information to the authorities.

The terms, conditions, and content of this website are governed by Vietnamese law, and the competent court in Vietnam will adjudicate.

3. Customer Rights

- You have the right to request access to your personal data, request us to correct any errors in your data at no cost. At any time, you also have the right to request us to stop using your personal data for marketing purposes.
